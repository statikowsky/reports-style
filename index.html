What this style is and isn't

Is

An editorial-feeling style for long-form analysis, plans, and design docs. Designed to read on screen as if it were printed — warm background, generous whitespace, restrained color.

Isn't

A UI framework. There are no buttons, forms, modals, or interactive states beyond the lcard hover. Don't try to build an app with this.
